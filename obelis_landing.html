Frequently Asked Questions

What is Obelis AI? +

Obelis AI is an intelligent platform that revolutionizes DevOps by using AI to deploy, estimate, monitor, and optimize your cloud services—all running on your own infrastructure with complete ownership and control.

Do you deploy to my infrastructure? +

Absolutely. Everything runs on your own infrastructure—we never host your workloads. You maintain complete control, security, and compliance while benefiting from AI-powered automation.

Can I bring my own tools? +

Yes! We seamlessly integrate with your existing CI/CD pipelines, observability tools, and infrastructure providers. No need to replace your current stack—we enhance it with AI intelligence.

Do I need DevOps experience? +

Not at all. Obelis AI handles all the complex DevOps heavy lifting—deployment orchestration, infrastructure provisioning, monitoring setup, and optimization. You focus on building great products, we handle the infrastructure.

How does AI improve my deployments? +

Our AI analyzes your application patterns, traffic, and resource usage to create optimal deployment strategies, predict and prevent issues, automatically scale resources, and continuously optimize for performance and cost.

Is my data secure? +

Security is our top priority. Since everything runs on your infrastructure, your data never leaves your control. We use enterprise-grade encryption and follow industry best practices for secure AI operations.
