Frequently Asked Questions

What is Obelis? +

Obelis AI is a 3-click deployment platform that lets you deploy to your own cloud provider account without any DevOps knowledge. Connect your GitHub repo, add AWS credentials, set environment variables, and deploy in under 5 minutes.

What happens to my AWS costs? +

You pay AWS directly for your infrastructure usage. Since everything deploys to your AWS account, you get full cost transparency and no vendor markup. This gives you AWS-level control at a fraction of the cost of managed platforms.

What applications are supported? +

We currently support static websites (React, Vue, Angular), Node.js, Python, Go applications and more. We also handle PostgreSQL and MySQL databases with automatic provisioning and backups.

Do I need DevOps experience? +

Not at all. Obelis AI handles all the complex DevOps heavy lifting‚Äîdeployment orchestration, infrastructure provisioning, monitoring setup, and optimization. You focus on building great products, we handle the infrastructure.

How is this different from Heroku or Vercel? +

Unlike Heroku or Vercel, you deploy to YOUR cloud provider account, meaning no vendor lock-in, full cost transparency, and complete infrastructure control. You get the simplicity of managed platforms with the power and economics of AWS.

Who is Obelis AI built for? +

Obelis AI is perfect for solo founders, indie developers, and small teams (2-10 people) who need to deploy applications but don't want to spend weeks learning DevOps. If you can build the app but deploying feels impossible, Obelis AI is for you.
